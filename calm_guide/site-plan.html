Overview

Purpose

This website will support people to medidate and relax. With short descriptions and tools to assist relaxation exercises.

Audience

The website will mostly seek to attend the needs for a young adult audience in college. However, it will be accesible and practical for most ages.

Dynamic elements

There will be a timer for the 4 to 4 breathing exercise, with a bar to follow along the breathing. There will also be a journal section for the user to report how they are feeling each day.

Home page

Content:

Peace, be still

Our Mission: In a world that moves quickly and demands constant attention, Calm Guide offers simple, accessible practices that nurture inner peace, mindfulness, and emotional balance. Whether you're just beginning or returning to moments of quiet, Calm Guide is here to provide guidance, encouragement, and resources that support your journey toward stillness — one breath, one pause, one step at a time.

How Can This Help Me? Calm Guide is here to help you find ease in your everyday life through small, meaningful habits. You’ll learn about the 4-4-4-4 breathing exercise, alongside a journal resource to express how you are feeling everyday. Whether you're feeling overwhelmed, curious about meditation, or simply craving a quiet moment for yourself, this site offers guidance without pressure, so you can return anytime you need.
